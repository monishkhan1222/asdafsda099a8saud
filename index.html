Our 6-D Process

Discover

Identifying your core audience, comparing what you say and do with what your competitors are doing, and analyzing how your target audience is searching for your products and services.

Ideas

It all starts with a simple idea but a good idea is not that easy to come up with. This is one of the most important phases of our cooperation. We’ll get to know you and your business better.

Concept

We’ll flesh out the idea in words, giving you a detailed concept & proposal. This proposal will highlight all aspects of working together – both the creative and the practical components.

Game Plan

Once we have your website audit, competitive analysis, defined buyer persona & mapped keywords. We create a game plan based on the chosen strategy, resources, and quarterly schedules.

Implementation

Implementation isn’t the last thing we’re going to do. Once we’re done, we’ll analyse the results of the digital marketing campaign and the manner in which people are perceiving your brand.

Optimization

Good is never good enough, especially when it comes to your content. We perfect the processes and refine the systems because we believe there is always a way to make things even better.
